Good to know
💰 Fijian Dollar (FJD)
~A$1 = FJ$1.60 · Cards accepted at resort. Bring some cash for village visits and tips.
🩺 Pack well
Reef-safe sunscreen is essential — even on cloudy days. Pack sea-sickness tablets for the ferry. August is outside cyclone season (Nov–Apr) so conditions are ideal.
👗 Dress code ashore
Cover shoulders and knees for village visits. A sarong (sulu) works perfectly — buy one locally.
